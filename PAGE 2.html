The Center Star

A Dynamic Star

From our vantage point on Earth, the Sun may appear like an unchanging source of light and heat. However, it is a dynamic star that is constantly changing and sending energy out into space. The study of these solar influences is known as heliophysics.

Solar Powerhouse

The Sun is a Yellow Dwarf star that acts as the primary source of light and heat for our solar system. It contains roughly 99.86% of the system's total mass and generates energy through nuclear fusion in its core.

Solar Characteristics

Classification G-type main-sequence star
Diameter 1.39 million km
Surface Temp ~5,500°C
Distance 150 million km (1 AU)
Current Age 4.6 billion years
Core Temp 15 million °C
