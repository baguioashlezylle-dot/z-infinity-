Creative Journey

This gallery serves as a cosmic collection of artistic works and projects completed for the English subject. Like the stars above, these stories provide light and meaning to our journey.

The Whistle

Benjamin Franklin

"When I saw any one too ambitious of court favour, sacrificing his time in attendance on levees, his repose, his liberty, his virtue, and perhaps his friends, to attain it, I have said to myself, This man gives too much for his whistle."

The Fall of the House of Usher

Edgar Allan Poe

"I know not how it was—but, with the first glimpse of the building, a sense of insufferable gloom pervaded my spirit... I looked upon the scene before me—upon the mere house, and the simple landscape features of the domain—upon the bleak walls—upon the vacant eye-like windows."
